AIR HANDLING UNIT

The CC has a high performance casing with a unique design meeting stringent thermal performance and hygiene demands. As standard, the CC unit meets the Eurovent T2, TB2 classification. The CC unit covers an airflow range from 2 000 to 70 000 m3/h (0.5 to 20 m3/s) as standard and up to 250 000 m3/h (70 m3/s) on special request. The functionality in the CC range includes a full range of heat recovery systems as well as the usual HVAC functionality. With the heat recovery systems and our range of high efficiency fans we are able to offer solutions with a low cost of operation while also reducing the energy consumption and the impact on the environment. The modular design allows you to configure the components in the way you need so that you can find the optimal use of space while fulfilling the technical requirements on air quality. The units are configured and accurately calculated in our Internet based configuration system Acon; which is Eurovent certified

Fan Coil Units

Fan Coil Units from Takyeef Factory LLC are available for installation in apartments, single or multi-room offices, schools, clinics, etc. Fan Coil Units from Takyeef Factory LLC are low noise, 3-speed units and available in varied configurations with many options and accessories. Deliveries on Fan Coil Units are very fast and reliable. Fan Coil Units are available in two different series, Hi-Static FCU (Standard and District Cooling) and Low-Static FCU (Standard and District Cooling). These are also available with EC motors.

Condensing Units

“CU series Air cooled condensing unit are designed for the use on systems with indoor units connected to remote condensing units located outside either on ground or on roof level. CU units are manufactured to meet the requirements of the gulf severe climatic conditions. CU Series Air Cooled Condensing unit are ideal for warehouses, large halls, schools, mosques, or wherever the requirement calls for a heavy duty unit with a scroll hermetic OR Semi Hermetic compressor. CU series Air Cooled Condensing units are used for commercial and industrial Applications where high efficient condensing units are needed to match an indoor central station AHUs or coils to get maximum performance advantages of split system with reasonable initial cost. CU series provide efficient operation, wide range of design flexibility coupled with packaged concept requiring least on-site work. The complete CU Series condensing unit provides an extremely rugged, heavy duty, long-life and energy efficient that will provide higher efficiency over a long and extended life. The Major Features are: • High efficiency totally sealed hermetic scroll compressor or Semi Hermetic compressor with or without capacity unloaders to match the required load. • Totally enclosed, Class F insulated, IP54 protected condenser fan motor. • Heavy duty condenser optimized in design for long-life maintenance free operation. • Cabinet construction specifically designed for Gulf climates. • IP 54 Electrical panel. The CU series air cooled condensing units are durable, dependable, strong, reliable, versatile, quiet and energy efficient. Wherever a heavy duty condensing unit is required, the CU series should be an automatic choice”
