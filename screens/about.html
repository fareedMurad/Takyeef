Takyeef Factory LLC is one of the Leading OEM Manufacturers and Marine, Oil & Gas HVAC Experts

Integrity , Safety, Innovation & Sustainability are the Four Pillars of our Core Values.

Takyeef Factory LLC is continuously improving its product design, services and its offering in order to meet our customer requirements and satisfaction. We are certifying our products with the highest HVAC standards. Our team is committed and dedicated to deliver complex and custom built solutions based on the different market specifications. We are present in differentiated Market segments including: Commercial building, Pharmaceutical Industry, Food Industry, Hospitals, Laboratories, Marine and Oil & Gas applications. The company operates a working environment that promotes the health and safety of its human capital and aim to develop their “know-how” in order to fully meet its customer needs. Takyeef Factory LLC offers superior quality HVAC products that are certified by globally recognised bodies like TÜV & UL .
